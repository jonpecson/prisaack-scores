Prisaack Scores

Experience Prisaack right in you mobile

This app is intended to be used for Prisaack Meet 2014, it features viewing of actual event schedules, live scoring updates and current school standings.

This app is made possible through the support of the whole KCC Community, it is intended to effectively disseminate information during Prisaack Meet.
